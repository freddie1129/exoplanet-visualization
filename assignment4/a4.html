  3. Are there any dangers of your final product possibly misleading the audience?

    4. The final product is less likely to mislead the audience. The final product actually is a highly interactive visualization, where the audience can select their interesting attribute and pick up their interesting range of exoplanets from the data collection.

    However, audience should understand some of visualization might not be able to reflect the real situation of the dataset due to the lack of data. This exoplanet collection has more than 3,000 exoplanets with 25 attributes, but not every attribute has valid value for a specific exoplanet. To draw the histogram for a specific attribute, I have to discard some exoplanets which has no data in this attribute. Therefore, the histogram is not the histogram for the entire dataset, but the sub-collection of the dataset. I think above issue is the only one that the audiences should realize when using this produce.
